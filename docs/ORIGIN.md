# Origin

Security Questionnaire Answer Studio exists after the scorecards, board brief, diligence pack, boardroom sparring, proof, and buyer-map layers.

The goal is to turn that executive-intelligence estate into one explicit answer-library and trust-evidence surface:

- which answer packs are ready to reuse now
- where the questionnaire or trust-evidence layer is still too weak
- what evidence is strong enough to support a buyer-safe diligence story
- which routes still need cleaner ownership and packaging before they can convert in security or legal review
